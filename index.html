About Me

I am an incoming graduate student at the School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University. I am currently working at TCS Research and Innovation Labs as a Researcher. I am a part of the Computer Vision and Deep Learning research group, working on the visual analysis of documents, and medical and cellular image analysis. Prior to this, I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi (IIIT Delhi), India. My research interests are in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Robotics.
